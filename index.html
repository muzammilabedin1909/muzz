About AD Events and Management

At AD Manpower Services, we are passionate about helping businesses and organizations achieve their staffing goals. With previous years of experience in the industry, we have a deep understanding of the staffing needs of companies of all sizes. Our mission is to provide quality manpower solutions that meet the needs of our clients, while providing career opportunities for our employees. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, based on trust, reliability, and excellent customer service. We are committed to providing our employees with the support they need to succeed in their careers, and to providing our clients with the best possible service. Whether you need temporary administrative support or a critical leadership role filled quickly, we have the expertise and experience to get the job done.

Temporary Staffing Solutions

When you need help quickly, our temporary staffing solutions can provide you with the support you need. Whether you're looking for an administrative assistant, a customer service representative, or a skilled tradesperson, we can help you find the right talent for your company.

Permanent Recruitment Services

If you're looking for a long-term solution for your manpower needs, our permanent recruitment services can help. We work with you to understand your business goals and the type of talent you're looking for, and then we use our expertise to find the right person for the job.

Contract Staffing Solutions

For businesses that have fluctuating manpower needs, our contract staffing solutions can provide you with the flexibility you need. Our contract staffing services allow you to bring on talent as needed, without the commitment of a permanent hire.

Payroll Management Services

Our payroll management services take the hassle out of managing your payroll. We handle all aspects of payroll processing, from calculating employee pay to producing pay stubs and W-2 forms.

Executive Search and Placement

When you need to fill a key leadership role, our executive search and placement services can help. Our experienced recruiters will work with you to understand your company's unique needs and find the right executive to lead your business to success.

Corporate Staffing

We can help you find the right people for your business, from administrative support to executive-level positions.
